COLOR SCHEME

The color scheme focuses on a visual hierarchy. It is based on an "Outdoor Adventure" theme, which consists of a greyscale palette for most elements, with a Yellow Green color to signify "Nature" and an Orange tone for call-to-action elements.
The following are some examples of how such colors will be used. They might change in the final design.
