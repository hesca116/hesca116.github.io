TARGET AUDIENCE DESCRIPTION

The target audience is comprised of people between 20 and 40 years old who like outdoor activities and are used to planning trips for their friends and/or family.
Such planning includes making sure the weather conditions are appropriate for the trip's planned activities.

PERSONAS

Persona 1

Persona Young Parents
Photo
Fictional name Mr Johnson
Demographics
  • 30 to 40 years old
  • Married
  • Children between 7 and 17
  • Works or lives near the countryside
Quote "I want to find places to go hiking with my family"
Scenarios
  • Has it rained through the past week on our camping site?
  • How will the weather be during our family hike this weekend?
  • Last time we went canopying the lines were closed due to bad weather, so this time I'll make sure in advance!
  • Can we take grandma to this national park? She doesnt like hot weather, so I'll find out the temperatures over there
  • Last time we went to that waterfall, the drive was difficult due to the thick fog, so I want to check the forecast for the mountain-side drive

Persona 2

Persona Young Adult Adventurer
Photo
Fictional name Coleen
Demographics
  • 20 to 30 years old
  • Single

  • Works or lives near the countryside

Scenarios

  • They close the dirtbike trail early when it rains, so I'll better check
