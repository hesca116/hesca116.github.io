Site name

Outdoor Genie

Site Purpose

To provide weather forecasts for outdoor-activity enthusiasts. Is the weather good for camping next weekend at a specific location? When will the cloudy weather clear to go sightseeing at a certain mountain top? That is the kind of information this website will provide.
The main feature might include comparing a weather forecast for a specific location with filtering by type of outdoor activity, or a search engine to find good spots for outdoor activities, based on their recent weather report and forecast.
